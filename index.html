Why this matters

Good preparation needs clarity.

  • People spend real money and still fail hard exams without the right kind of guidance.
  • Application rules and official structures should be easy to find, not hidden behind noise.
  • Strong learners deserve a study home that is practical, calm, and serious about the work.
In honor

Sleep medicine grows because mentors keep the door open.

Sleep Pathways Guild honors William C. Dement, MD, PhD, for helping shape the modern field of sleep medicine and its culture of serious teaching, and Robert P. Dopson, RPSGT, for the generous mentorship spirit that welcomed learners instead of turning them away.

The site is guided by that spirit: serious teaching, steady support, and respect for the work of learning.
