Sleep Pathways Guild

A guild for the craft of sleep technology.

Sleep Pathways Guild is a professional, peer-led community for credentialed sleep technologists and CCSH practitioners, focused on mastery of monitoring, signal interpretation, and real-world practice. Built in the spirit of a guild, it exists to support structured learning pathways, guided study, mentorship, professional development, and shared expertise in a craft-centered, standards-driven environment.

Forever-free resource

Free Self-Guided Webapp for RPSGT Exam Preparation

Created by Sleep Pathways Guild, this independent educational resource is designed to help learners prepare for the Registered Polysomnographic Technologist™ (RPSGT) credentialing exam administered by the Board of Registered Polysomnographic Technologists (BRPT).

Free, permanently

This specific RPSGT study webapp is intended to remain free so cost is not the reason a serious learner lacks a structured place to begin.

  • Free guided study access
  • Free practice and review tools
  • Free skill-lab entry points
Open the RPSGT Exam Webapp

Built for readiness

The webapp emphasizes both memory and judgment: knowledge recall, best-answer thinking, weak-area repair, and exam-oriented practice.

  • Guided study trail
  • Practice questions and mock-exam flow
  • Reports, review direction, and skill labs

Independent and respectful

It is designed to point learners toward official resources, high standards, and strong source habits—not to replace credentialing authorities or textbooks.

  • Original practice materials
  • No claim of official endorsement
  • Verify current official rules and requirements directly

Independent and standards-aware

A clear note about the Guild and its free self-guided webapp for RPSGT exam preparation.

Sleep Pathways Guild is an independent professional community and educational project. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or approved by BRPT, AASM, AAST, textbook publishers, media companies, or other organizations unless specifically stated.

The Sleep Pathways Guild webapp for RPSGT exam preparation contains original educational practice materials, study pathways, rationales, labs, and teaching visuals created for self-guided review. It does not reproduce actual BRPT examination items or proprietary exam questions, and it does not promise or guarantee any exam, credentialing, employment, or clinical outcome.

Learners should verify current credentialing requirements, exam policies, blueprint details, scoring rules, clinical standards, and official references directly through the appropriate official sources.
